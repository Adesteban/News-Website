Five dead in fire in Corinthian Gardens, QC


Published March 20, 2021 6:56am

Five people died in a fire that broke out in a residential area in Corinthian Gardens, Barangay Ugong Norte, Quezon City before dawn on Saturday.

The fatalities are members of the Yu family, whose house burned down, according to a report by Luisito Santos on Dobol B TV.

Four of the fatalities' bodies were burned. One died due to suffocation, the Bureau of Fire Protection (BFP) said, according to a report on News TV Live.

Those who died were identified as Gilbert Yu, Ryan Yu, Charito Yu, Abigail Yu and Rita Yu.
Two others were injured, also members of the Yu family.
The fire which reached the second alarm started on the second floor of the Yu house, the BFP said.
